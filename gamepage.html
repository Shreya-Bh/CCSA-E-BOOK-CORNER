MAZE GAME:

Rules for playing the game:

1. Objective of the game is to Navigate from the starting point to reach the endpoint or goal of the maze.

2. Use keyboard arrow keys or mouse controls to move through the maze.

3. Walls and obstacles block certain paths. Navigate around them to find the correct route.

4. Successfully reaching the goal acknowledges the player's win.

5. Players have the option to restart the maze to attempt it again.
